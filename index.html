General Introduction


Santosh Mahapatra has completed his Ph. D. in English Language Teaching at the Department of English, University of Hyderabad. In his Ph. D., he looked into how a need-based teacher education programme shapes teachers' ability to carry out assessments. Before joining BITS-Pilani, Hyderabad, he taught MCA and Integrated Masters students at the University of Hyderabad and worked as a Teaching Assistant at the English and Foreign Languages University, Hyderabad. His research interests are Language Assessment, ESP, Teacher Education, Web 2.0 Tools and Critical Pedagogy. Santosh is a movie-buff, likes doing photography, cooking and cycling.

About


This website is made as a replacement/alternative to the Course Management System, except it caters to the course "Creative Thinking (GS F326)" only. I have used HTML, CSS, W3-CSS, Javascript and Jquery in this website. Even though I am a web-developer I made sure that I implement enough web-design techniques to make it as aesthetic as possible. The title page with its simplistic design and the main page with its color choices are evidences of the above fact.

The website contains a Query/Grievance form which is unique as it doesn't use backend but still delivers the required message to the Instructor in Charge. I have e-mailed the IC a google sheet which will be updated as soon as the form is filled. This involves combination of Google APIs and sheets.

The website also uses Google calender tweak which helps in adding the class schedule directly to your calender. It is a feature of Google calender implemented through APIs.

I have used "000webhostapp" to host the website. Since it is a free web hosting service it has a default downtime of 1 hour (3 am to 4am).
